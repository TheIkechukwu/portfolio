## 🌟 Featured Projects

### 🍲 [Igbo Dish Classifier](https://igbodish.streamlit.app/)
A culturally grounded deep learning app that identifies Igbo dishes from images using **ResNet18 + FastAI**, then uses **Gemini AI** to serve up real-time history, ingredients, and preparation details. Part technology, part cultural preservation.

---

### 🔍 [WhatsApp Sherlock](https://whatsappsherlock.streamlit.app/)
An AI-powered WhatsApp chat analyser that uncovers your messaging patterns — activity peaks, top words, emoji usage, and sentiment. Like having a personal detective for your conversations.

---

### 🩺 [Diabetes Prediction Model](https://github.com/TheIkechukwu/TheIkechukwu/blob/main/Classification%20model.pdf)
A classification model that predicts diabetes likelihood from medical features, achieving **97% accuracy** and a remarkably high F1 Score. Built with rigorous feature selection and model evaluation.

---

### 🍷 [Wine Quality Classifier](https://github.com/TheIkechukwu/TheIkechukwu/blob/main/wine-quality-classification.ipynb)
Multiple classification models benchmarked against each other to predict wine quality from physicochemical properties. A clean study in model comparison and selection.

---

### 📉 [PCA & The Curse of Dimensionality](https://github.com/TheIkechukwu/TheIkechukwu/blob/main/simple-classifier.ipynb)
An applied exploration of Principal Component Analysis — demonstrating how smart dimensionality reduction improves classifier performance on high-dimensional datasets.

---

### 💼 [Data Scientist Job Market Analysis (2023)](https://github.com/TheIkechukwu/Analysis-of-Data-jobs-using-SQL)
A SQL-powered deep dive into 2023 data science job postings — surfacing top-paying roles, most in-demand skills, and the optimal skill combinations for salary and demand. Career intelligence, data-style.
